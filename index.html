The workout is completed when all 52 cards have been dealt.

Dinner Picker answers the age old question: "What's for dinner?" With Dinner Picker, coming up with new and healthy meals is easy - just roll the dice. Roll 1 die for protein, 1 for your main "body" veggie or starch, 1 for an accent veggie, 1 for your cooking method, and the last for your flavor profile. Dinner's all planned and almost on the table! This single-page application was created with a combination of HTML, CSS and jQuery. All images and copy are mine.
